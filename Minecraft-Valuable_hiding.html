How to hide valuables quickly & efficiently in Minecraft


If you're playing on a server in Minecraft, then there will always be someone who wants to steal your valuables
such as your Emeralds, Gold, Diamonds, and Netherite. Today, let's look at some of the clever ways to hide valuables.


1. Unexpected areas

Everyone would think that there is nothing under the ground. Start by crafting your valuables into blocks. Dig out a small 2-block-deep patch in the ground. Fill 1 layer of the patch with your valuables. Then repair the rest of the patch with dirt. No one would ever know it was there!

2. Under the cauldron

Many players use cauldrons as a sink. It would be less unlikely for a player to hide valuables under the cauldron. Put a barrel under the cauldron because a chest would be obstructed by the cauldron. With only very less gap between the cauldron and the ground, players would not notice the barrel.

3. In the furnace

Most players have a smelting room- to smelt ores. In one furnace of yours, put the valuables in the output slot. No player would go checking the furnaces- not even one!
